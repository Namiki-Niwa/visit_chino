Suwa Lake Summer Nights

Throughout the event, fireworks light up the sky every evening, offering spectators the opportunity to leisurely enjoy the display from the lakeside grass without overcrowding. Guests can marvel at the magnificent fireworks synchronized to music in the evening breeze. The Summer Night Fireworks Cruising (Fireworks Night Cruise) adds a romantic touch to each evening during the event period.

Suwa Lake Firework Festival

Featuring a unique waterborne spectacle, including dazzling waterborne starmines, the fireworks display boasts an overwhelming scale. Fireworks launched from platforms set up on the lake surface adorn the summer night sky. Moreover, as Suwa Lake is surrounded by mountains on all sides, the echoes of the fireworks resonate throughout the mountains.

2nd - September - 2024

Suwa Lake Firework New Festival

Lake Suwa boasts one of the nation's premier locations for launching and viewing fireworks, attracting tourists from around the world. Against the backdrop of Lake Suwa, talented pyrotechnicians from across Japan compete in a competition showcasing innovative and original fireworks creations, breaking free from traditional constraints and highlighting artistic expression.
